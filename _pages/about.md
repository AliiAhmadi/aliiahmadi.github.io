I am a last-year undergraduate student in Computer Science at the University of Tehran. My research interests are Artificial Intelligence, Computer Vision, Human-Computer Interaction, Reinforcement Learning and Machine Learning. Currently, I am an active member of the Deep Learning Laboratory at the University of Tehran, where I engage in research projects at the intersection of AI and real-world impact.

Beyond computer science, I have a deep passion for mathematics. I am especially drawn to Graph Theory and Abstract Algebra, both for their beauty and for their foundational role in theoretical computer science.
